About us.

The first 1000 days of a child are very important! The window of opportunity opens during this time, that is why at Sakha Intsha Learning Centre, we ensure that your child receives an education of genuine quality, through inclusive education. It is important that communication exists between the centre and parents to ensure that the focus always remains on what is beneficial for your child as an individual.

Our aims

Nature and provide a stimulating learning experience in a safe and happy environment.
Improve the quality of basic education and laying a solid foundation in the early years.
Develop the young leaner as a whole.
